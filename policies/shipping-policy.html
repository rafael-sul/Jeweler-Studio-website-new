Shipping

Every Jeweler Made order ships fully insured via FedEx 2-Day with adult signature required. We do not leave fine jewelry at doors.

Domestic (United States)

Complimentary on all orders. In-stock pieces ship within 3-5 business days of purchase. Made-to-order and custom pieces follow the production timeline communicated at order; ground / 2-day shipping is added at no charge once the piece is ready.

International

We ship worldwide via FedEx International Priority. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout. The customer is responsible for any import duties, taxes, or customs fees levied by their destination country.

Production timelines

  • Ready-to-ship pieces — ship within 3-5 business days.
  • Made-to-order from the catalog — typically 3-4 weeks.
  • Fully custom pieces — typically 4-6 weeks from design approval.

Tracking

Once your order ships you'll receive an email with the FedEx tracking number. All shipments require an adult signature on delivery. If no one is available to sign, FedEx will hold the package at the nearest location for pickup.

Insurance & loss

Every package is fully insured for its declared value through FedEx. In the very rare case a package is lost or damaged in transit, contact us immediately and we'll handle the claim and remake the piece at no charge.
