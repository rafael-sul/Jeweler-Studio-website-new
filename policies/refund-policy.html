Returns & refunds

We want you to love the piece you've commissioned. Here's how we handle changes.

Ready-to-ship catalog pieces

Returnable within 14 days of delivery for full refund, less original shipping if international. Pieces must be returned in original condition with all packaging and certifications. Once we've received and inspected the return, your refund is issued within 5 business days to the original payment method.

Made-to-order & custom pieces

Because we make these pieces specifically for you, custom and made-to-order work is non-refundable. We will, however:

  • Offer one complimentary resize or minor adjustment within 30 days of delivery.
  • Re-make or repair anything that arrives different from what we agreed in the design phase, at no charge.
  • Discuss redesign options if the piece doesn't suit and you'd like to apply credit toward something new.

Engraved or personalized work

Engraved pieces are final sale. We'll always send a final proof of the engraving for your written approval before we cut it.

Damaged in transit

If a piece arrives damaged, photograph it before unwrapping further and email hello@jewelermade.com within 48 hours. We'll arrange a replacement or repair at no charge.

How to start a return

Email hello@jewelermade.com with your order number and a brief reason for return. We'll send a pre-paid, insured FedEx return label. Do not ship valuables without insurance.
